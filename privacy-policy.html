Privacy Policy

Audience Track prioritizes the privacy of our users. This privacy policy document outlines the types of information that is collected and how it is used.

Information We Collect:

  • Public TikTok Data: We collect publicly available data from TikTok videos, such as video titles, descriptions, and comments. This data is used solely for the purpose of analysis and providing insights into TikTok content.
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect any personal information, such as email addresses, phone numbers, or user credentials, unless explicitly provided by the user for specific interactions with the app.

How We Use Your Data:

  • We use the data collected from TikTok for educational and research purposes.
  • We do not share or sell any of the data collected with third parties.

Data Retention:

Data retrieved from TikTok is only stored temporarily in the app for analysis and is not retained beyond its immediate use.

Security:

We take reasonable precautions to ensure the security of the data we handle, but we cannot guarantee 100% security.

Your Rights:
